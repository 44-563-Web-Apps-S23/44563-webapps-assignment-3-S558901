Saffron Tiger

Saffron Tiger, the tiger is the largest wild cat in the world. The big cat's tail is three feet long. On average the big cat weighs 450 pounds, about the same as eight ten-year-old kids. It stands three feet tall with teeth four inches long and claws as long as house keys.

Saffron Tiger' powers
  1. Super strength
  2. Super Jump
  3. Strong teeth
